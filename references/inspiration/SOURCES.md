## How to Use These

1. **Download 2-3 reports** from the consulting section
2. **Study the first 5 pages** — cover, TOC, exec summary, first data page, first chart
3. **Note the ratios** — how much whitespace vs content, how large are stat numbers vs labels
4. **Compare with our templates** in the `reports/` subfolder to see how we match up
5. **Look at what they DON'T do** — no gradients on every element, no 5 colors per chart, no cramped margins

The goal isn't to copy any single report. It's to internalize the shared principles: restraint, hierarchy, breathing room, and letting data speak.
